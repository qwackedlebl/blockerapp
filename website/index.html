How It Works

1

Download & Install

Download the APK and install it on your Android device. Grant the necessary permissions (Accessibility, Overlay, Usage Access).

2

Get Your Secret Key

Your device automatically generates a unique secret key. Find it in "My Secret Key" section.

3

Share With Your Partner

Give your secret key to a trusted friend who will be your accountability partner. They'll add it to their RotLocker app.

4

Lock Distracting Apps

Go to "My Apps" and toggle the lock switch for any apps you want to block (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.).

5

Stay Accountable

When you try to open a locked app, you'll need a code from your accountability partner or the Super Secret Key to unlock it permanently.
